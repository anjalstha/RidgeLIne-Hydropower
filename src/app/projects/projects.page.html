Featured Project


Super Chepe Hydropower Project


The Super Chepe Hydropower (SCHP) Project is a run-of-the-river type hydropower project based on Chepe River in Ajirkot Rural Municipality, Gorkha district of Nepal. The installed capacity of the project is 9.05 MW and annual average energy is 54.75 GWh. The project is being developed by Ridge Line Energy Ltd. and financed by Rastriya Banijya Bank Ltd. with 70:30 loan:equity ratio.

Featured Project


Dudhpokhari Chepe Hydropower


Ridge Line Energy Ltd. is a major investor of Dudhpokhari Chepe Hydropower (DPCH) project. DPCH is a run-of-river type hydropower project with installed capacity of 8.8 MW. It is being developed by Dudhapokhari Chepe Hydropower Ltd. in Chepe Khola of Ajirkot Gaupalika, Gorkha District. It has estimated annual energy of 56.19GWh.
