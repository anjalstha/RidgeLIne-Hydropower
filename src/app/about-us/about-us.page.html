About the company

Ridge Line Energy Ltd. is a renewable energy company registered at the office of the company registrar, government of Nepal. The company is led by a by group of young and competent board members supported by a professional technical and managerial projects members to design, construct and operate hydropower projects.

Ridge Line Energy (RLE) Limited was established in 2074 BS with an objective to work on energy sector in Nepal. Currently, RLE is developing Super Chepe Hydropower Project (SCHP) as its flagship hydropower project with installed capacity of 9.05 MW and is also a major investor of Dudhpokhari Chepe Hydropower Project with installed capacity of 8.8 MW. Both projects are based on Chepe River, Ajirkot Rural Municipality, Gorkha District in western Nepal.
